Privacy Policy — 90+ Draft

Last updated: June 10, 2026

90+ Draft ("the App") is a football draft and tournament simulation game developed by Facundo Vogel ("I", "me", "the developer"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or registration is required.
  • No name, email address, or contact information is collected.
  • No location data is accessed or stored.
  • No usage analytics or crash reports are sent to any server.
  • No advertising identifiers are used.
  • No data is shared with third parties.

2. How the App Works

All game data — including player databases and match simulation logic — is bundled within the App itself and processed entirely on your device. The App does not require an internet connection to function and does not communicate with any external server.

3. External Links

The App contains an optional link to the developer's Ko-fi page (ko-fi.com/cacuuv), where users may choose to leave a voluntary message or support the project. Tapping this link opens an external website in your browser. The developer is not responsible for the privacy practices of Ko-fi. Visiting that page is entirely optional and has no effect on the App's functionality.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected at all, the App is safe for users of all ages.

5. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes in the future, the updated version will be published at this URL. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
